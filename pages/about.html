About us

This website provides awareness information,Quizzes and games to help you get to know more about Eco Park Education through a educational programs and ecological displays. Eco Park Education is a website that fosters human connection with the natural world. Environmental educators, naturalists who are committed to ensuring that everyone has access to sustainability make up our team.

History

Eco Park Education began with three students from Edusn Digital School who wanted to create a simple website focused on protecting local green spaces, plants, animals, and natural habitats. Over time, this small idea grew into a full educational platform that offers interactive learning experiences for students, families, and visitors. Today, it continues to inspire people to explore nature and take action for a more sustainable future.

Vision

To empower communities to take meaningful action toward a sustainable future, creating a significant environmental impact by using interactive learning tools, inspiring eco-friendly behaviors, and revitalizing local parks to serve as vibrant, educational spaces for everyone.
