Eco Park Education

Offers fun activities and games that show simple ways to reduce waste, save resources, and help the planet.

Why should people care about Eco Park?

Preserves local nature and wildlife while giving people a safe space to learn and enjoy nature.

How does Eco Park Education help visitors?

Teaches visitors, especially teenagers, how to protect the environment and adopt sustainable habits.

OUR AIM

Creating A Sustainable Park Website

We aim to design an interactive and meaningful tool that guides visitors to understand sustainability while discovering better ways to improve and redesign our local park.

What is Eco Park?

Eco Park is designed to protect nature, save the environment, and give people a clean place to enjoy. It protects wildlife, uses eco-friendly materials, reduces pollution, and promotes healthy outdoor activities.

Sustainable Park Tips

Bring Your Own Bottle

Avoid single-use plastics. Carry a reusable water bottle to stay hydrated and protect the park environment.

Clean Up After Yourself
